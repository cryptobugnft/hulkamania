Hulkster Whitepaper

A Tribute to Hulk Hogan (1953-2025)

Introduction

Hulk Hogan, a global icon in professional wrestling, passed away on July 24, 2025, leaving behind a legacy of charisma and strength that inspired millions. The Hulkster token is created to honor his memory, offering a unique digital asset that connects fans and admirers to his storied career through the Cashtoken BCH chain.

Token Details

  • Token Name: Hulkster (HULK)
  • Description: Hulkster (HULK) is a cryptocurrency token launched on the Cashtoken BCH chain to commemorate Hulk Hogan, who passed away on July 24, 2025. This token embodies his iconic strength, charisma, and enduring legacy, providing fans and collectors a digital tribute to his contributions to professional wrestling.
  • Symbol: HULK
  • Total Supply: 1,000,000,000 HULK
  • Blockchain: Cashtoken on Bitcoin Cash (BCH) Chain

Token Distribution

  • Team: 10% (100,000,000 HULK) - Allocated to the project team, subject to a 12-month vesting period.
  • Liquidity: 20% (200,000,000 HULK) - Reserved for liquidity pools on BCH-compatible exchanges.
  • Airdrop: 70% (700,000,000 HULK) - Distributed to the community to engage fans and supporters in honoring Hogan’s legacy.

Technical Overview

Built on the Bitcoin Cash (BCH) blockchain with the Cashtoken upgrade, Hulkster ensures secure and low-cost transactions. The token leverages BCH’s Proof-of-Work consensus and scalable infrastructure.
