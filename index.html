Hulkster Whitepaper

A Tribute to the Legend Hulk Hogan

Introduction

Hulk Hogan, a global icon in professional wrestling, has left an indelible mark on entertainment with his charisma and strength. The Hulkster token is created to honor this legacy, offering a unique digital asset that connects fans and admirers to his storied career through the power of blockchain technology on the Cashtoken BCH chain.

Token Details

  • Token Name: Hulkster (HULK)
  • Description: Hulkster (HULK) is a cryptocurrency token launched on the Cashtoken BCH chain to pay tribute to the legendary wrestler Hulk Hogan, affectionately known as the "Hulkster." This token embodies his iconic strength, charisma, and enduring legacy, offering fans and collectors a unique digital asset to celebrate his contributions to professional wrestling.
  • Symbol: HULK
  • Total Supply: 1,000,000,000 HULK
  • Blockchain: Cashtoken on Bitcoin Cash (BCH) Chain

Token Distribution

  • Team: 10% (100,000,000 HULK) - Allocated to the project team, subject to a 12-month vesting period.
  • Liquidity: 20% (200,000,000 HULK) - Reserved for liquidity pools on BCH-compatible exchanges.
  • Airdrop: 70% (700,000,000 HULK) - Distributed to the community to engage fans and supporters.

Technical Overview

Built on the Bitcoin Cash (BCH) blockchain with the Cashtoken upgrade, Hulkster ensures secure and low-cost transactions. The token leverages BCH’s Proof-of-Work consensus and scalable infrastructure.

Legal and Compliance

Hulkster will comply with relevant cryptocurrency regulations. Users are responsible for adhering to local laws regarding token ownership and trading.
